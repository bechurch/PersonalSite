What Have I Done?

I have a B.Sc in Computer Science from the University of Victoria with a specialization in HCI and Software Engineering. I have worked professionally as a Web Developer at Socoloco deploying both frontend and backend features to Diggit.ca, Gobid.ca and Begiving.ca. Before that I had been a Quality Assurance Engineer at Semadic and Skype, and a Web Designer for both Code-Rite and Ghana Futures a charity I was heavily involved with.

I told you! I love to build! If you're looking for a more in depth description of my work feel free to to checkout my resume, github page or linkedIn.
